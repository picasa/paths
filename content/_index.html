A visual blog about algorithmic art, mainly designed for pen plotters.

I am using the R programming language to write algorithms that generate digital outputs. Rather than printing, I am interested in using a pen-plotter to draw them on paper. While this process enables drawing with enhanced repetition and precision, it limits the use of fills or line width variation.

This site presents digital and some paper outputs, and documents the ideas underlying the functioning of the algorithms. Most of the code to study and reproduce these outputs is released here with an open license. Overall, making visual art is very new to me, in my day job I am a scientist working in plant biology. Feel free to contact me via email or Twitter for any questions related to this blog.
